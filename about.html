The Creative Soul

Dynamic Stories Studio was founded on the belief that every person, every brand, every moment holds a story worthy of being told beautifully — and that it's our profound honour to be the ones to tell it.

Our Beginning

A studio born from
a single obsession:
truth in light.

In 2009, founders Aria Menon and Marcus Webb shared a tiny New York loft, a secondhand Hasselblad, and an audacious belief — that photography could be simultaneously art and emotion, craft and soul, commerce and poetry.

What began as late-night sessions and borrowed equipment, shooting friends for the love of the frame, evolved into a studio that today works across four continents with the world's most iconic brands and the most intimate of personal commissions.

"We never wanted to just photograph what we see. We wanted to photograph what we feel."

Today, Dynamic Stories operates with a team of twelve devoted visual artists united by one conviction: that the most powerful photograph you'll ever see is the one that makes you feel something you didn't know you were carrying.

What Drives Us

Our Values

01

Authenticity

We believe every person and every brand has a genuine story. Our role is to discover and reveal it — never manufacture it. Authenticity cannot be styled into existence; it must be earned through trust.

02

Mastery

Technical excellence is non-negotiable. We obsess over light, composition, timing, colour science, and post-processing until every pixel earns its place. Great art begins with great craft.

03

Emotion

A photograph that doesn't make you feel something has failed, regardless of technical perfection. We pursue emotional resonance above all else — the image that stops you in your tracks at 3am.

04

Legacy

We create for eternity, not for trend. Every image we produce should remain meaningful, moving, and visually powerful decades from now. We are stewards of permanence in a disposable age.

Our Journey

A Decade of
Milestones

Founded
2009

New York Studio Opens

Aria Menon and Marcus Webb establish Dynamic Stories in a SoHo loft. First editorial published in New York Magazine. The journey begins with $2,000 and an unreasonable amount of ambition.

First Award
2012

World Press Photo — First Prize

Marcus Webb receives First Prize at World Press Photo for "The Last Light of Lagos" — a documentary series on resilience in West Africa. International recognition transforms the studio overnight.

Global Expansion
2016

London · Mumbai · Dubai

Dynamic Stories expands globally with three new studios. First collaborations with Hermès, Condé Nast India, and the Dubai Design District. The team grows to twelve visual artists.

Recognition
2020

IPA Grand Prize & Studio of the Decade

Aria Menon receives the IPA Grand Prize for portraiture. Aperture Magazine names Dynamic Stories "Studio of the Decade." A 60-print retrospective opens at the ICP in New York.

Present Day
2024

1,200+ Clients Across 40 Countries

62 international awards. 3,800+ projects delivered. A global community of 180,000 photography students through our masterclass programme. Still obsessed with the perfect frame.
